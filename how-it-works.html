How this repo works

A practical, self-contained toolkit for planning beautiful websites — built by RavenClaude.

What is DesignFlow?

DesignFlow is an interactive, multi-step HTML form that helps you (or your clients) clearly define a website project. It guides users through content strategy, branding decisions, site structure, and technical requirements — then outputs a clean, structured JSON blueprint + human-readable design brief.

The goal is simple: remove ambiguity before any design or development begins. When you finish the form and export the JSON, you (or RavenClaude) have everything needed to build the real site accurately and efficiently.

How to Use the Interactive Form

1 Open the Form

Open index.html in any modern browser. No build step, no dependencies — it just works.

2 Work Through the 6 Steps
  • • Project basics & goals
  • • Content prompts & messaging
  • • Branding & visual direction
  • • Site structure & sitemap
  • • Features & integrations
  • • Review, preview & export
3 Export Your Blueprint

In Step 6 you can download a clean JSON file or copy the Design Brief. This becomes the single source of truth for the project.

4 Build the Real Site

Share the JSON with RavenClaude (or your developer). We use it to generate production HTML, Tailwind components, full pages, or even a complete custom website tailored to your inputs.
